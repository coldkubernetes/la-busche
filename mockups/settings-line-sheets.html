What changed & why

  • No nav Sync/Language/Backup aren't a separate screen, tab, or drawer at all — they open as a bottom sheet over your route list, the same overlay pattern this app already uses for the Dropbox restore prompt and import confirmation. You never leave Setup, so there's no "two levels deep" feeling.
  • Always there The three stops sit in a thin bar directly under the header, outside the scroll area — they're in view the instant you open Setup and stay there no matter how many routes you scroll through.
  • Signature The bar is drawn as a short transit line with stops, not a generic chip row or icon strip — a quiet nod to what this app is actually for, instead of an off-the-shelf nav pattern.
  • Status The Sync stop carries a small green dot when connected and synced, so you can tell at a glance without opening anything.
  • Same content Each sheet holds exactly what's in the current Dropbox/Backup/Language sections today — only where they live changed, not what's in them.
